- Track a simple regime framework (trend, momentum, volatility) to guide adjustments:

6) Monitoring and triggers
- Weekly check-ins on:
  - Breadth indicators (which stocks are leading, number of advancing vs. declining stocks).
  - Trend/momentum signals (price relative to 50/200-day, MACD/Hull momentum signals, RSI).

- Explicit exit/entry triggers:
  - If SPY breaks below a key trend line with weakening breadth, scale hedges up or trim further.
  - If breadth broadens and macro data improves, reduce hedge coverage incrementally and increase net exposure.

- After each major event (Fed, CPI, earnings season), perform a quick post-mortem to see if hedges performed as expected and whether the regime signals were timely. Adjust hedging rules and exposure bands accordingly.
