# Investment Report: QCOM
## Executive Summary
Qualcomm Incorporated (QCOM) stock is facing potential risks and challenges, making it essential to exercise caution when investing. While the company has a strong track record of innovation and competitive advantages, concerns about its stock performance, competitiveness, and underlying risks cannot be ignored.

## Investment Decision
**Sell**

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision

* The integration of Apple-related assets may pose significant costs and complexities for QCOM.
* Relying too heavily on innovation as a strategy can lead to uncertainty and volatility in the company's stock price.
* QCOM may struggle to replicate its past success, particularly if it fails to adapt to changing market trends.

## Risks To Watch

* Integration of Apple-related assets: The costs and complexities associated with integrating these assets could negatively impact QCOM's financial performance.
* Dependence on innovation: If QCOM relies too heavily on innovation as a strategy, it may struggle to maintain its competitive position in the face of changing market trends.
* Competition from emerging technologies: QCOM faces increasing competition from emerging technologies, which could erode its market share and profitability.
