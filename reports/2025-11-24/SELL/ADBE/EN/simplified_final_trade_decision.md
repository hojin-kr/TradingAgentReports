# Investment Report: ADBE
## Executive Summary
Adobe's strong financial health and growth potential are offset by concerns over the Figma acquisition, competition from emerging players, and global economic uncertainty.

## Investment Decision
SELL with a confidence level of 60%

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
- The Figma acquisition poses significant risks due to its impact on Adobe's business model and competitive landscape.
- Global economic uncertainty and competition from emerging players threaten Adobe's growth potential.
- Despite the benefits of innovation, these risks outweigh the positive indicators.

## Risks To Watch
- The success of Figma integration: If Adobe struggles to integrate Figma into its existing products, it may hinder growth.
- Competition from emerging tech companies: New entrants in the market could challenge Adobe's market share and pricing power.
- Global economic downturn: A recession or slowdown in global economic growth could impact Adobe's sales and profitability.
