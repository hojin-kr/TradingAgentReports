- Regulatory/headwinds: Antitrust scrutiny could raise costs or constrain monetization.
- Ad-cycle/macro sensitivity: Heavy reliance on ads can affect earnings visibility.
- Cloud competition and costs: Pricing pressure and higher data-center spend could constrain margins.
