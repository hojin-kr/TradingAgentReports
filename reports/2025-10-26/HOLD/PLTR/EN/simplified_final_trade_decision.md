# Investment Report: PLTR
## Executive Summary
Hold Palantir today. The bull case is plausible (durable platform, potential Lumen cross-sell, AI-driven margins), but near-term data on commercial ARR growth, gross margins, and cross-sell velocity are unclear, and regulatory/government exposure adds risk.

## Investment Decision
Recommendation: Hold. Confidence level: Medium.

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
- Data gaps limit timing confidence: key metrics like ARR growth, gross margins by mix, and cross-sell velocity are not yet clear.
- Valuation risk: stock is valued high relative to near-term profitability and ARR pace.
- Regulatory/government exposure: government contracts and potential policy changes raise volatility and risk.
- Durable platform and governance: the core platform features (security, governance, data integrity) support long-term stickiness, especially in regulated industries.
- Potential upside signals exist: partnerships (e.g., Lumen) and AI platforms could lift margins and support ARR growth if deployment accelerates.
- Path to operating leverage: as commercial bookings grow, there is a plausible path to higher margins and better cash flow, though this is not yet proven.

## Risks To Watch
- ARR growth and cross-sell momentum: slower-than-expected commercial expansion or weaker cross-sell could hurt the outlook.
- Margin expansion uncertainty: near-term gross margins may not improve as hoped.
