# Investment Report: TSLA
## Executive Summary
Tesla's stock price may be due for a correction due to its competitive weaknesses and uncertain business model.

## Investment Decision
Sell

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
* Rivian and Lucid Motors' growing presence in the electric vehicle market threatens Tesla's dominance.
* Elon Musk's leadership style is unpredictable, causing short-term volatility.
* Tesla's business model relies on government subsidies and tax credits, which may not be sustainable.

## Risks To Watch
* Tesla's lower operating margin compared to other companies in the industry.
* Uncertain product pipeline and lack of competitive advantages.
* Potential for long-term decline due to changing market trends.
