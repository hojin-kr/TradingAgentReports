# Investment Report: NFLX
## Executive Summary
Netflix (NFLX) faces challenges from competitors like Disney+ and HBO Max, but its strong brand recognition and original content offerings provide a competitive advantage.

## Investment Decision
SELL

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
* Competition from streaming services is significant, and NFLX may struggle to maintain market share.
* Financial health, pricing, and earnings are valid concerns that shouldn't be ignored, particularly due to its reliance on international markets and relatively small market share in certain regions.

## Risks To Watch
* Continued competition from Disney+ and HBO Max could erode NFLX's market share and revenue growth.
* International market challenges and relatively small market share in certain regions may impact financial health and earnings.
