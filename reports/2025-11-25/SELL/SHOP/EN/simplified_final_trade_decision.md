# Investment Report: SHOP
## Executive Summary
Shopify's growth potential is threatened by significant risks from supply chain disruptions, inflation concerns, and over-reliance on e-commerce. We recommend selling SHOP stock due to these risks.

## Investment Decision
Sell SHOP stock

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
* Supply chain disruptions could severely impact Shopify's operations and revenue.
* Inflation concerns could reduce consumer demand for Shopify's services.
* Over-reliance on e-commerce growth makes Shopify vulnerable to changes in consumer behavior.

## Risks To Watch
* Supply chain disruptions: Shopify's reliance on third-party logistics providers increases the risk of supply chain disruptions, which could impact its ability to deliver products to customers.
* Inflation concerns: Rising inflation could reduce consumer demand for Shopify's services, particularly if consumers become more price-sensitive.
* Over-reliance on e-commerce growth: Shopify's business model is heavily dependent on e-commerce growth, making it vulnerable to changes in consumer behavior and economic trends.
