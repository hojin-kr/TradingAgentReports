# Investment Report: NKE
## Executive Summary
Nike Inc.'s (NKE) stock is not a good investment opportunity due to declining sales, high debt, and reliance on the sneaker market.

## Investment Decision
SELL

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
NKE's decline in sales and high debt burden increase the risk of default and reduced profitability. The company's reliance on the sneaker market as a growth driver may not be sustainable due to intense competition in the footwear industry.

## Risks To Watch
* Declining sales: NKE's sales have been declining, which could impact its revenue and profitability.
* High debt burden: The company's high debt levels increase the risk of default and reduced profitability.
* Reliance on the sneaker market: NKE's growth is heavily dependent on the performance of the sneaker market, which is subject to intense competition and changing consumer trends.
