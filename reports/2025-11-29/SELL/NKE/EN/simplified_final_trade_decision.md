# Investment Report: NKE
## Executive Summary
Nike's stock price has been volatile, with recent headwinds affecting its performance. We recommend selling NKE due to concerns over its growth potential and increasing competition.

## Investment Decision
SELL with confidence level 8/10

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
NKE's recent struggles are largely due to increased competition in the athletic wear market. The company faces challenges in maintaining its market share, and its growth prospects have been impacted by this. Additionally, NKE's stock price has been subject to significant volatility, making it difficult to predict future performance.

## Risks To Watch
NKE's stock price could be affected by:

*   Continued competition from other athletic wear brands
*   Changes in consumer spending habits and preferences
*   Economic downturns or recessions that impact the retail industry
