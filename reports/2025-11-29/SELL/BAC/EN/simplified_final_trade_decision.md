# Investment Report: BAC
## Executive Summary
BAC's significant weaknesses and potential risks outweigh its revenue growth and market share, making it a less attractive option for investment.

## Investment Decision
SELL with confidence level 8/10 due to the bank's dependence on legacy business, regulatory challenges, and competition from fintech firms.

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
- BAC's reliance on its legacy business creates significant risks.
- Regulatory challenges pose a threat to the bank's operations.
- Competition from fintech firms erodes market share.

## Risks To Watch
- Continued dependence on legacy business.
- Increased regulatory scrutiny.
- Growing competition from fintech firms.
