# Investment Report: AMZN
## Executive Summary
Amazon (AMZN) faces significant risks, including a high valuation, slowing growth rate, and increasing competition from other tech giants. We recommend selling the stock at or below $200 to limit potential losses.

## Investment Decision
SELL with confidence level 80%

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision

* High valuation: Amazon's price is relatively high compared to other tech giants.
* Slowing growth rate: The company's growth rate has been slowing down, which may indicate a decline in its future prospects.
* Increasing competition: Other tech giants are entering the e-commerce and cloud computing markets, increasing competition for Amazon.

## Risks To Watch

* Continued rise in valuation: If Amazon's valuation continues to rise beyond $240, it could lead to a sharp decline in its stock price.
* Failure to innovate: If Amazon fails to innovate and adapt to changing market trends, it may struggle to maintain its market share.
