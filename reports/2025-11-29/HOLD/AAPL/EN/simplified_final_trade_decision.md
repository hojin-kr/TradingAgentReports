# Investment Report: AAPL
## Executive Summary
Apple's stock price may be due for a correction as supply chain disruptions and high valuation multiple pose significant risks. A cautious approach is recommended to protect assets.

## Investment Decision
HOLD

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
* Diversified supply chain reduces risk of supply chain disruptions
* Limited room for growth presents opportunities for underappreciated growth
* High valuation multiple is already factored into the stock price
* Opportunities for underappreciated growth exist, especially with Apple's strong track record of innovation

## Risks To Watch
* Supply chain disruptions risk due to reliance on Chinese suppliers
* Potential risks associated with global economic downturn and high valuation multiple
* Limited room for growth could lead to stagnant earnings
