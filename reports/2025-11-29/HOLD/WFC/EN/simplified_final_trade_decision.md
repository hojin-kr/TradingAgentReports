# Investment Report: WFC
## Executive Summary
Wells Fargo & Company (WFC) stock is recommended for sale due to significant risks and concerns associated with investing in the company.

## Investment Decision
BUY, SELL

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
- **Regulatory Issues**: WFC's history of regulatory issues and potential future changes in the mortgage lending space pose a risk to its operations and profitability.
- **Cybersecurity Concerns**: The company's cybersecurity vulnerabilities could lead to significant losses or disruptions.
- **High Debt Levels**: WFC's high debt levels increase the risk of default or financial instability.

## Risks To Watch
- Regulatory changes in the mortgage lending space
- Cyber attacks on the company's operations
- High debt levels and potential for default
