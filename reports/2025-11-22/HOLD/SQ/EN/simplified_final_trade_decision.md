# Investment Report: SQ
## Executive Summary
Square Inc. (SQ) faces significant challenges, including regulatory uncertainty, competition from established players, and high cash burn rates. While the company has strong fundamentals and innovative products, its growth potential is uncertain.

## Investment Decision
RECOMMEND SELL

## Key Reasons Supporting This Decision
* Regulatory uncertainty presents a challenge for SQ's operations in new markets.
* Competition from established players could limit SQ's ability to grow.
* High cash burn rates raise concerns about the company's profitability and cash flow.

## Risks To Watch
* Regulatory changes in the payment processing market could impact SQ's business.
* Increased competition from established players could reduce SQ's growth potential.
* High cash burn rates could lead to reduced profitability and cash flow.
